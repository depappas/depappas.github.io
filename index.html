Our Services

We offer sports consulting services that are designed to take players, coaches, and teams to the next level.

We design training programs custom taliored to the needs of the players, coaches, and teams which we serve. For players we assess the player's current levels of game intelligence, skills, and understanding and ability to execute patterns. For coaches we consult on training programs and strategy. For teams we work on developing better understanding between players, with a focus on attacking and defensive patterns of play and scoring.

Palyer Intelligence

We teach the 6 step player intelligence model using real training on the field. Perception, conception, decision, deception, execution, and assessment

Offensive Skills

Basic, intermediate, and advanced skills are taught with a focus on biomechanics, footwork, posture, and game situation to use the skills.

Defensive Skills

Defensive skills are taught with an emphasis on tackling, marking, channeling/shadowing, and tackling in pairs.

Offensive patterns

Offesnive patterns are taught for defensive, miodfield and attacking buildup play as well as plays designed to generate shots on goal

Defensive Patterns

Defensive patterns are taught which include defensive formations, organization, and running lines.

Drag flick training

The biomechanics and footwork of the drag flick are taught in stages. See the Drag Flack book for an idea of the depth of training.

Formations, tactics and Strategy

Customized formations, tactics, and stragey are developed for teams based on level, opposition, and the desired style of play
